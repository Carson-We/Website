BMI Calculator

The BMI Calculator is a handy tool designed to calculate your Body Mass Index (BMI). It supports both metric (kg/cm) and imperial (lbs/in) units, allowing you to enter your height and weight in the unit system you prefer.

By using this calculator, you can easily determine your BMI, which is a measure of body fat based on your height and weight. It helps you assess whether you are underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.
